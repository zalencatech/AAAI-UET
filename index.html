AAAI — The Global Parent

The Association for the Advancement of Artificial Intelligence (AAAI) is one of the world's leading organizations dedicated to advancing AI research and responsible practice. Founded in 1979, AAAI drives global discourse on AI through conferences, journals, and student chapters worldwide.


aaai.org · Since 1979

🏛

AAAI-UET Body — Our Chapter

The AAAI Student Chapter at the University of Engineering & Technology, Lahore is a community of AI-passionate engineers, researchers, and creators. We bridge the gap between academic AI theory and real-world application — right here on campus.


UET Lahore · Est. 2024

Pakistan's Premier AI Student Community

To become the leading platform for AI education and innovation at the student level in Pakistan — a breeding ground for researchers, engineers, and entrepreneurs who will define the future of AI in South Asia.
